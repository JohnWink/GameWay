Overwatch: Lunar New Year 2019

24/01/2019

A new Seasonal Event has arrived to Overwatch!

Get ready to ring in the Year of the Pig!


Blizzard Entertainment is celebrating Lunar New Year for a third year in Overwatch, a seasonal event that will run from Jan. 24 to Feb. 18. This time, it’s the Year of the Pig, and players can gorge on new skins for characters like Tracer, Reinhardt, Reaper, Brigitte, and, yes, even Orisa.

For Overwatch Lunar New Year 2019, Blizzard is adding eight new skins — six legendary, two epic. Five of those skins are based on Chinese military generals, strategists, and warlords from the country’s Three Kingdoms period: Guan Yu (Reinhardt), Lü Bu (Reaper), Huang Zhong (Hanzo), Zhang Fei (Torbjorn), and Zhuge Liang (Zenyatta). Orisa gets a skin, Sanye, based on the Korean lion dance costume. Brigitte gets some ornate Korean general garb. And Tracer gets a skin based on Korean folklore hero Hong Gildong.
